ARE INCOME LEVEL AND HOMICIDE CONNECTED?

For our Group Project, we wanted to know: how likely is it that a person might be a victim of homicide, based on their income?
To find out, we analyzed FBI and US Census Data, looking specifically at median income and unemployment levels for both men and women.
